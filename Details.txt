Life Book:
Description:
Life book is an productive app for every user, User can plan & schedule the goals, reminders, and the plan settings here are, how to implement them to achieve the life’s goals.

Features:
•	Add Tasks into the App.
•	User Interaction & Support.
•	Delete the task.
•	Add Reminder
•	Delete Reminder.
•	Add the data and Check or Uncheck.
•	Task to do. 
•	Fully Different User Interface Designed keeping in focus point of view of Niche (Target) Market.
•	 Live Tiles(Locally & Remotely)
•	Searching feature.
•	Fully Unique Branding (Unique Logo Designing & Graphics Designing).
